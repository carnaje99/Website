About One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese shōnen manga series written and illustrated by EiichiroOda. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine since July 22,1997, and has been collected into 96 tankōbon volumes as of April 2020. The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gained the properties of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit. With his crew of pirates, named the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy explores the Grand Line in search of the world's ultimate treasure known as "One Piece" in order to become the next King of the Pirates.
